Traditional Foods of Andhra Pradesh

Pesarattu

A nutritious green gram dosa, usually served with ginger chutney or upma. It’s a breakfast favorite in Andhra.

Pulihora

A tangy tamarind rice dish tempered with curry leaves, mustard seeds, and peanuts — a staple at festivals and temples.

Andhra Chicken Curry

Spicy and flavorful chicken cooked with local spices — a signature non-veg dish of Andhra cuisine.

Gongura Pachadi

A famous chutney made from sorrel leaves, known for its tangy and spicy flavor — often eaten with hot rice and ghee.

Ariselu

A traditional sweet made from rice flour and jaggery, deep-fried in ghee — a festive delicacy, especially during Sankranti.
