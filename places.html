Tourist Places in Andhra Pradesh

Tirupati

Home to the world-famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, Tirupati is a major pilgrimage destination attracting millions of devotees every year.

Undavalli Caves

These 4th-century rock-cut caves near Vijayawada are known for their impressive architecture and monolithic statues carved into sandstone.

Visakhapatnam Beach

Popular for its scenic coastline, RK Beach in Vizag is ideal for sunrises, leisure walks, and local seafood. It's a favorite weekend destination.

Kanaka Durga Temple

Located on Indrakeeladri Hill in Vijayawada, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga and is a key religious and cultural site.

Lambasingi

Also known as the "Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh," Lambasingi is a cool hill station known for misty mornings and scenic beauty.
