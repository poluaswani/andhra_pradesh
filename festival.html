Festivals of Andhra Pradesh

Ugadi

Ugadi marks the Telugu New Year and is celebrated with traditional food, new clothes, and cultural programs.

Sankranti

A harvest festival celebrated with kite flying, bonfires, and traditional sweets like ariselu and pongal.

Dasara (Vijayadashami)

Celebrated with devotion and grandeur, especially in Vijayawada, where Goddess Kanaka Durga is worshipped.

Diwali

The festival of lights celebrated with lamps, fireworks, and sweets across the state.

Krishna Pushkaralu

A sacred river festival that occurs once every 12 years on the banks of the Krishna River.
